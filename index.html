INTERFERENCE SERIES “Since 2020, due to the Corona pandemic, we working on smaller-scale projects, and instead of producing one large public event, we work year-round featuring residencies, workshops, study, and research programs. In 2020/2021, we realized DIGITAL SHIFT in international cooperation with the Digital Impact Lab Bremen and supported by Institut for Foreign Affairs (ifa) in Germany, ELECTRICAL DISORDER in cooperation with the Heinrich-Boell-Foundation Tunis; the YOUNG MASTERS PROGRAM was supported by the Goethe-Institut Tunesien.” Partners of international exchange were Cairotronica in Cairo (eg), Media Art Friesland in Leeuwarden (nl), Svetlobna Gverila in Ljubljana (si), the LICHTSTROM Festival in Ingolstadt (de), and the UNDARK Festival in Yekaterinburg (ru)—Come visit us.
